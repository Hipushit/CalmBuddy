About CalmBuddy

CalmBuddy is your gentle, uplifting app for anyone who feels anxiety or discomfort using public transport. Whether you’re afraid of panic on the subway, uncomfortable on a bus, or simply need support to make your journey easier — CalmBuddy is always by your side.

Key Features

  • SOS Button: Get instant, real-time support from community members who will accompany you online through your journey — you’re never alone.
  • Soothing Nature Tracks: Calm your mind with birdsong, ocean waves, rain, or forest sounds.
  • Funny Memes & Podcasts: Enjoy uplifting memes and short podcasts made for anxiety relief.
  • Breathing Techniques & Guided Meditations: Quick exercises and meditations with a caring guide, anywhere, anytime.
  • Victory Journal: Celebrate your small wins — "took the bus alone," "arrived at work calmly," and more.
  • Anonymous, judgment-free chat: Get or give encouragement, always safe, always confidential.

Why does it work?

  • Real-time support — "emotional Uber" for anxious moments
  • Completely anonymous and non-judgmental
  • Simple, friendly, non-clinical community
  • Extra tools: checklists, guided breathing, positive audio
  • Supporting others reduces your own anxiety too

Karma Points — Earn Kindness, Get Rewards

Every time you support someone — answer an SOS, send an encouraging message, share your story, or simply celebrate someone’s small victory — you earn Karma Points.

  • Unlock new nature soundtracks and exclusive meditations
  • Special meme packs just for buddy-helpers
  • Unique “Victory Badges” for your CalmBuddy profile
  • Fun self-care mini-games and kindness tips
  • Participation in special giveaways and surprise rewards

On CalmBuddy, being kind pays off — for your soul and your app experience.
